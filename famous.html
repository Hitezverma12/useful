Famous People

Meet a few of India’s most celebrated figures: from freedom fighters and scientists to artists and entrepreneurs who shaped modern India.

Meet the Icons

Mahatma Gandhi

The leader of India’s independence movement, famous for nonviolent resistance and a global symbol of peace.

Kalpana Chawla

The first Indian-born woman in space, whose legacy inspires a generation of scientists and explorers.

Ratan Tata

A business leader and philanthropist who helped build Tata Group into a global icon and invests in ambitious social innovation.

Mother Teresa

Honored worldwide for her humanitarian work in Kolkata, she dedicated her life to serving the poor and sick.

APJ Abdul Kalam

The "People's President" and rocket scientist, known for pioneering India’s missile program and inspiring youth.

MS Dhoni

One of India’s most successful cricket captains, celebrated for calm leadership and clutch performances.
