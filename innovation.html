Engineering Highlights Main Page

Space Missions

India’s space agency ISRO has made headlines with Chandrayaan, Mangalyaan, and reusable launch tech that compete globally.

Startup Ecosystem

India is home to thousands of startups in fintech, healthtech, edtech, and AI, backed by a vibrant founder community.

Research Growth

Universities and labs are building momentum in biotech, quantum computing, and climate science research.

Innovation Highlights

  • ISRO launched Chandrayaan and Mangalyaan missions at world-class cost efficiency.
  • India became the world’s third-largest startup ecosystem, with many unicorns.
  • Smart cities, digital payments, and Green Hydrogen are shaping tomorrow’s economy.

What Makes It Unique

  • Frugal engineering creates powerful tech at lower cost.
  • Innovation is built on deep cultural diversity, local talent, and global ambition.
  • Collaboration with universities, private companies, and public research labs drives rapid change.
