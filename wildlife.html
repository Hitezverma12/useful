Bengal Tiger

India protects more than half the world’s wild tigers. National parks like Ranthambore and Bandhavgarh are the best places to spot these majestic cats.

Asian Elephant

From Assam’s forests to Kerala’s backwaters, elephants roam protected corridors and appear in cultural traditions across India.

Birdlife

India is a birder’s paradise with thousands of species, including cranes, kingfishers, and migratory visitors in wetlands like Keoladeo and Chilika.
